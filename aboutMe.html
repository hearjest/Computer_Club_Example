Who are you?

My name is Brian. I like programming and Japanese. Programming is what I hope to be my career , but it'd be cool if Japanese got involved somehow.

Why programming and Japanese?

Well I like the sheer amount of possibilities programming can give me. It allows me to help myself and others, but I'm still very much a beginner. I also wanted to know how dropdown menus worked.
As for Japanese, I'd watched a ton of anime while I was young and I thought "man, wouldn't it be cool to understand Japanese without reading subtitles?" and here I am. I've started studying Japanese March~ish of sophomore year of highschool.

What do you hope for in the future?

I'm happy enough getting decently paid at some web development or something programming oriented job. I really just want to live financially stable while also getting the chance to invest into programming and Japanese more.
